The Caverns of Kalte

Footnotes

[1] (Section 4) All other Bone Swords in this book are recorded as normal Weapons, but the Collector’s Edition notes that this is a Kalkoth rib blade which adds 1 to your COMBAT SKILL when used in Kalte.

[2] (Section 61) This peculiar ending is the only time in the Lone Wolf series where you can fail your mission without dying. Despite your survival, you have not completed this adventure and may not continue on to future adventures.

[3] (Section 132) According to the Collector’s Edition: ‘If you currently possess enough food for 1 Meal, you may now consume it in order to avoid this ENDURANCE point deduction.’

[4] (Section 139) The Laumspur Potion referred to here is specifically referred to as Red, which indicates a special concentrated Potion. A regular Laumspur Potion does not qualify.

[5] (Section 350) The portal you throw Vonotar into is known as a Shadow Gate. This particular Shadow Gate leads to the Daziarn, a realm from which no one has ever returned.
